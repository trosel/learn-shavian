The letter for so is 𐑕. Yep, it looks like a Latin S :)

The letter for if is 𐑦. It looks like an i without the dot :)

The letter for eat is 𐑰. This sound is similar to "if", except it is longer. This letter looks like someone tried to lengthen the 𐑦 and it broke in the middle!

The letter for they is 𐑞.

Try to sound out what words these make. Don't click until you've figured it out!

The letter for zoo is 𐑟. Yes, it's a reversed 𐑕! Similar to the Latin s z. 𐑕 𐑟.

Compare these letters together: 𐑕 𐑟. Pronounce each of them: so, zoo. Notice that the zoo letter is lower on the line. You can remember that "zoo" actually sounds lower in your mouth than "so" as well.
