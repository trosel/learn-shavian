The letter for dead is 𐑛. Yep, it's an upside down "tot"! Just keep in mind that "tot" is "top" so it points up ( 𐑑 ) and "dead" is in the ground so it points down ( 𐑛 ).

The letter for roar is 𐑮. This is a backwards "loll" of course. Again, remember that when you make an "r" sound, your tongue is pointed further back in your mouth. The "l" sound is at the front of your mouth so it points forward: 𐑤 𐑮.
