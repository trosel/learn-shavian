The letter for bib is ğ‘š. Yes, it is sort of like an upside-down peep! See them together: ğ‘ ğ‘š.

There is a common pattern in Shavian where similar sounds have a similar look to them. You may not have realized it before, but "peep" and "bib" are indeed similar. Make a "b" sound and blow air through your lips. You just made a "p" sound! Notice that bib ( ğ‘š ) does feel a bitter lower when you say the sound. So keep in mind that it is the lower letter on the line too.
